Introduction

I am a senior student in the School of Software, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am a research member at the Digital ART Lab of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I have also conducted research at Cascade Lab in University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign.

My research interests lie in the fields of Human Computer Interaction and Game Design and Technology. I'm fascinated with applications related to Virtual Reality and Mixed Reality. I'm hoping to build novel interactive interfaces in a playful and aesthetic way.

In my spare time, I am a rapper and I create my own music! I am constantly writing new songs, recording and producing music. Recently I am learning composing and making beat. Check out some of my songs here.
I am often invited to give performances on and off campus. Check out some of my performances here.

Feel free to contact me via Email, Github, and Facebook.
