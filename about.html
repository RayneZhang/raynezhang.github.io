Introduction

I am a senior student in the School of Software, Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am a research member at the Digital ART Lab of Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I have also conducted research at Cascade Lab in University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign.

My research interests lie primarily in the fields of Human Computer Interaction, Game Design and Mixed Reality. I design interfaces, especially games, to help people gain better experiences and improve their lives. I'm hoping to build novel interactive interfaces in a playful and aesthetic way. You can check out some of my projects/portfolio here.

In my spare time, I am a rapper and I create my own music! I write, record and produce music in my studio. You can check out some of my songs here. I am often invited to give performances on and off campus. Check out some of my performances here.

Feel free to contact me via Email, Github, and Facebook.
