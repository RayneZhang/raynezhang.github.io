I am a senior student in the School of Software at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. I am a research member at the Digital ART Lab of Shanghai Jiao Tong University, supervised by Xubo Yang and Shuangjiu Xiao. I have also conducted research at Cascade Lab in University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign, supervised by Wai-Tat Fu.
My research interests lie primarily in the fields of Human Computer Interaction, Mixed Reality and Game Design. I combine my love of computer science, music and art to create novel interactive technologies. I hope to work on combining mixed reality and game design with qualitative and quantitative research methods to help people gain better experiences and strengthen their abilities.
